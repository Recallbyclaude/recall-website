About

Your contacts app,
rebuilt around memory.

Recall Contacts is an iPhone app that lets you find anyone in your contacts by what you remember — not how their name is spelled.

Tag people by context. #NYC #Eastwood #crazyhat. Search by hashtag and find them instantly. No spelling required.

Smart colour-coded groups. Swipe-to-tag for rapid organising. A dyslexic-friendly mode. Your tags sync back to Apple Contacts — your data stays yours.

Search by #hashtag

FAQ
Does Recall replace my iPhone contacts app?
No. Recall layers on top of Apple Contacts. Your Phone app stays as is. Recall is for finding and organising.
Do my tags sync across devices?
Tags are stored locally, but Recall writes them back to the Apple Contacts notes field — so they sync via iCloud Contacts if enabled.
What happens if I delete Recall?
Your hashtags stay in Apple Contacts. Your data doesn't disappear.
Is Recall really free?
Yes. Free to download. No subscription. No account required.
Is my contacts data private?
Completely. Nothing leaves your device. Recall never connects to any external server.
Is there an Android version?
Currently iOS only. iPhone and iPad are supported. Mac Catalyst brings it to Mac.
