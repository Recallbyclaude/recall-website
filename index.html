About

Your contacts app,
rebuilt around memory.

Recall Contacts is an iPhone app that lets you find anyone in your contacts by what you remember — not how their name is spelled.

Tag people by context. #NYC #Eastwood #crazyhat. Search by hashtag and find them instantly. No spelling required.

Smart colour-coded groups. Swipe-to-tag for rapid organising. A dyslexic-friendly mode. Your tags sync back to Apple Contacts — your data stays yours.

Features

Everything Recall does.

Search by #Hashtag
Type what you remember — a place, a moment, a relationship — and Recall finds the right person instantly. No spelling required.
Swipe to Tag
Add hashtags to contacts in one swipe. A card-style flow makes bulk tagging fast — swipe right to tag, left to skip.
Smart Groups
Every hashtag becomes a live Smart Group automatically. Tap #NYC to see everyone tagged with it. No manual management ever.
Super Tags
Five colour-coded priority tags built in from day one: VIP, Family, Work, Parents, Friends. Your most important people, always visible.
Quick Recall
One-tap access to Call, FaceTime, Message, WhatsApp, Email, or Share. The right action is always one tap away.
Browse by Last Used
Sort contacts by recency to keep active relationships at the top. Your native Contacts app can't do this. Recall can.
Parent Mode
Attach children's names to school parent contacts. Search #Luca to find that child's parent instantly. You know the kid — Recall connects you to the adult.
Private by Design
No account. No email required. Your data stays in Apple Contacts via iCloud — on Apple's servers, not ours. Delete Recall and your tags remain.
Mac & iPad Support
Runs natively on Mac via Catalyst and iPad. Full keyboard navigation on Mac. One purchase — iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

FAQ
Does Recall replace my iPhone contacts app?
No. Recall layers on top of Apple Contacts. Your Phone app stays as is. Recall is for finding and organising.
Do my tags sync across devices?
Tags are stored locally, but Recall writes them back to the Apple Contacts notes field — so they sync via iCloud Contacts if enabled.
What happens if I delete Recall?
Your hashtags stay in Apple Contacts. Your data doesn't disappear.
How much does Recall cost?
$2.00 — one-time purchase. No subscription. No account required.
Is my contacts data private?
Completely. Nothing leaves your device. Recall never connects to any external server.
Is there an Android version?
Currently iOS only. iPhone and iPad are supported. Mac Catalyst brings it to Mac.
