Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Recall Contacts respects your privacy.

Recall Contacts is designed to help you save and find people using contextual information such as tags, notes, roles, places, events, projects, and optional photos. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Access

Recall Contacts may access information that you choose to use within the app, including:

  • contact information from your address book
  • notes, tags, and other context you add to contacts
  • photos or selfies you choose to attach or reference
  • basic device permissions needed for contacts and photos access

How Information Is Used

Recall Contacts uses this information only to provide the app's core functionality, including:

  • helping you create and organize contacts
  • writing tags or context into your existing contacts
  • helping you search and find people later using the details you remember
  • displaying linked photos or selfies as memory cues

Data Storage

Recall Contacts is designed so that your contact-related information remains under your control.

Your contacts, tags, notes, and related context are stored on your device and within your own contact system unless explicitly stated otherwise inside the app.

  • Recall Contacts does not sell your personal data.
  • Recall Contacts does not use your contacts, notes, tags, or photos for advertising purposes.
  • Recall Contacts does not upload your personal contact data to remote servers unless a future feature clearly asks for your permission and explains what is being shared.

Photos

If you choose to take or attach a selfie or photo, that image may be saved to your Photos library or linked to a contact record, depending on how the feature is used. These images are used only to help you remember the person and context of the meeting.

Third-Party Services

Recall Contacts does not share your personal contact information with third parties for marketing or advertising.

If third-party tools or services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

Your Choices

You control what information you add to Recall Contacts.

You may also manage or revoke app permissions for Contacts and Photos at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Recall Contacts is not intended to collect personal information from children independently of a parent or guardian. The app is a contact utility and does not include social networking, public posting, or advertising features.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised effective date.
