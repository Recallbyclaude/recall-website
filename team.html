The people behind it

Meet the team

Paul Barbera
Creator, Recall Contacts

Paul is a photographer with 25 years of editorial, commercial, and documentary work behind him. His portraits and stories have appeared across mainstream media — spanning fashion, music, culture, and the human faces that connect them all. Dyslexic, probably ADHD, and perpetually surrounded by people he could picture but couldn't spell — Paul built Recall because he needed it. The result is an app designed for the way real memory works, by someone who spent a career living the problem.

Jason Laan
Co-founder, Laan Labs

Jason is co-founder of Laan Labs, a boutique app studio he built with his brother Christopher over nearly two decades. In that time they've shipped some of the most downloaded apps in App Store history — including Face Swap Live, the original live face-swapping app and a global #1 hit, and Tap DJ, a professional mixing app built around their signature GyroScratch™ technology. Jason knows what it takes to build apps people actually want, and he brought that experience to Recall from day one.
